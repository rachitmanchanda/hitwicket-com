The grandest Esports competition for cricket!

Hitwicket Superstars, a Mobile Cricket Strategy Game, where the user is the Owner, Coach and Captain of a virtual cricket team. Gamers in Hitwicket Superstars are transported to a magical cricketing realm where they own cricket players with superpowers. They must train their players - from rookies to superstars who command a diverse range of superpowers - from enabling them to hit 12 runs in one ball to conceding negative runs to the opposition.

Our vision is to marry the mass appeal of Cricket with the accessibility of mobile gaming to create an immersive experience that delights a billion cricket fans worldwide. A World-Class Cricket Strategy Game - made in India, for India and the world.

Format

Climbing up the steps and reaching the doorsteps of the Cricket Metaverse World Cup is no less than a thrilling journey. With head-to-head Alliance Cricket Matches and Individual Cricket Wars, Hitwicket World Cup is all set to bring the top cricket superstars on a single platform. All you need to do is, advance through the Qualifiers to set your feet into the World Cup.

The World Cup will occur over 4 stages:

  • Pre-Qualifier
  • Alliance Wars
  • Live Qualifiers
  • World Cup Finals

Qualifier

Win challenging matches against the opponents & stand a chance to stretch your winning form in the World Cup. The Pre-Qualifiers would be divided into two sections: Open Qualifiers & Closed Qualifiers, each of six phases.


Open Qualifiers
  • Check-in limit of 2048 Alliance Teams
  • One-Day Competition
  • Random Seeding
  • Knock-out Rounds
  • Top-64 teams will advance to the Closed Qualifiers.

Closed Qualifiers
  • Top-64 teams from Open Qualifiers
  • One-Day Competition
  • Random Seeding
  • Knock-out Rounds
  • Top-4 teams will advance to the Live Qualifiers

In-Game Alliance Wars
  • 10-days Competition
  • Top-4 teams will advance to the Live Qualifiers.

Live Qualifiers
  • Top-4 teams from Closed Qualifiers + Top-4 teams from Alliance Wars
  • 8 teams will be divided into 2 groups of 4 teams each
  • Two-Day Competition
  • Double Elimination
  • Top-4 Teams will advance to the World Cup Playoffs
